What's a Barcamp? 🤔

It's an unconference where YOU create the agenda! Bring topics you want to discuss, problems you're solving, cool tech you want to share, or projects you're working on. We'll organize sessions on the spot based on what interests everyone.

  • Vote & cluster - We'll group similar topics together
  • Self-organize - Choose sessions that interest you most
  • Share & learn - Everyone contributes, everyone benefits
